<h1>Highlights</h1>
<p></p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/null-50.png" width="41" height="41" alt="" title=""></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Coach Tip: </b>This is a handy tool for you to use while reviewing students’ comments on the Metrics page.</li>
<li><b>Student Tip: </b>You can save a quote or copies of your comments to your list of highlights as you are completing activities in the path.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>While working on your Path, you may come across something interesting or important that you want to flag for future reference. You have the ability to save text by using the Highlights feature. You can save a comment, a quote, or an excerpt from your <b>Path</b>.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>The Highlights feature can be used in both the <b>Metrics view</b>, the <b>View all Replies</b> page and within the <b>Path</b> itself.</p>
<p>You can see your saved <b>Highlights</b> by viewing them on your personal <b>Highlights</b> page.</p>
<p>To access the <b>Highlights</b> tool, click on the main navigation menu in the top left hand corner of your screen.</p>
<p></p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/null-51.png" width="68" height="109" alt="" title=""></p>
<p></p>
<p>Click on the <b>Highlights</b> menu</p>
<p></p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/null-52.png" width="156" height="292" alt="" title=""></p>
<p></p>
<h2>Creating and Saving a Highlight in Paths</h2>
<p>To add <b>Highlights</b> from your Path, simply select a block of text from an Activity and then click the <b>Highlight</b> button which will appear in the bottom left of the screen.</p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/null-53.png" width="328" height="48" alt="" title=""></p>
<p>You will notice the <b>Save Highlight </b>modal<b> </b>comes up automatically with your highlighted text in quotations. </p>
<p>Select at least one checkbox, choosing the <b>Folder</b> where you want to save the text.</p>
<p>Press <b>Save Highlight. </p>
<p><b><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/null-54.png" width="624" height="390" alt="" title=""></b></p>
<p>You can also add a new Folder by clicking on the word <b>Add</b>.</p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/null-55.png" width="273" height="141" alt="" title=""></p>
<p>Type in the name of the folder. Press Create.</p>
<p>Press <b>Save Highlight.</p>
<h2>Creating a <b>Highlight</b> in the Metrics Page</h2>
<p>Coaches can also create <b>Highlights</b> while they are reviewing and responding to students’ comments inside the <b>Metrics</b> page.</p>
<p>As you are reviewing a students’ responses to an activity, select the portion of the student’s comment that you would like to save as a highlight.</p>
<p></p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/Select20text20in20user20path20content20in20the20Metrics20tool20to20show20the20launcher20bar20for20highlights..png" width="617" height="182" alt="Select text in user path content in the Metrics tool to show the launcher bar for highlights." title=""></p>
<p>Click the Highlight button in the <b>launcher bar</b> to load the highlight tool.</p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/Select20text20in20user20path20content20in20the20Metrics20tool20to20show20the20launcher20bar20for20highlights..jpeg" width="400" height="100" alt="Select text in user path content in the Metrics tool to show the launcher bar for highlights." title=""></p>
<p>Select at least one folder to add the highlight to.</p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/Select20text20in20user20path20content20in20the20Metrics20tool20to20show20the20launcher20bar20for20highlights.-1.png" width="584" height="205" alt="Select text in user path content in the Metrics tool to show the launcher bar for highlights." title=""></p>
<p>Click <b>Save Highlight</b> to add a new highlight.</p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/Select20text20in20user20path20content20in20the20Metrics20tool20to20show20the20launcher20bar20for20highlights.-2.png" width="468" height="140" alt="Select text in user path content in the Metrics tool to show the launcher bar for highlights." title=""></p>
<h2>Sharing with teams</h2>
<p>If you are saving a highlight of a student’s comments, you can only share it with a team which both you and that student belong to.</p>
<p>When you create the highlight, only the mutual teams will be shown.</p>
<h2>Viewing Highlights</h2>
<p>View a list of your saved highlights by clicking on the main navigation menu in the top left corner of your screen.</p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/null-51.png" width="68" height="109" alt="" title=""></p>
<p></p>
<p>Click on <b>Highlights</p>
<p></p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/null-56.png" width="318" height="197" alt="" title=""></p>
<p></p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/null-57.png" width="624" height="96" alt="" title=""></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Highlights page is divided into two sections: <b>My Folders</b> and <b>Teams</b>. </p>
<p>Any highlight stored inside the <b>My Folders</b> section is private and can only be viewed by you. </p>
<p>When you save a highlight under the <b>Teams</b> section, it can be viewed by coaches on that team.</p>
<h3>My Folders</h3>
<p>Everyone begins with a single folder called <b>Personal</b> under the <b>My Folders</b> heading. </p>
<h3>Creating and Deleting custom folders</h3>
<h3>Creating a folder</h3>
<p>You can add a new Folder by clicking on the word <b>Edit</b>.</p>
<p>You will then be in the Edit mode.</p>
<p>Type in the name of the folder. Press <b>Create</b>.</p>
<p>When you are finished creating folders, press <b>Done Editing.</p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/null-58.png" width="392" height="149" alt="" title=""></p>
<p>Your new folder will appear at the top of the list of folders.</p>
<h3>Deleting a folder</h3>
<p>You can also delete a custom folder by clicking on the word Edit. </p>
<p>You will then be in the Edit mode.</p>
<p>Click on the trash icon located to the right of the folder’s name. </p>
<p>This message will appear, making sure you truly want to delete a folder and any saved <b>Highlights</b> in the folder.</p>
<p>Press OK to delete.</p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/null-59.png" width="485" height="157" alt="" title=""></p>
<p>When you are done making changes, click on the <b>Done Editing</b> link to leave the Edit mode.</p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/null-60.png" width="393" height="201" alt="" title=""></p>
<p>Any folders you’ve created in the <b>Highlights</b> page will be available in the list of folder choices when you Save a <b>Highlight</b> in the future. </p>
<p>When in Edit mode, <b>Highlights</b> can also be moved to different folders.</p>
<h3>Managing Team Highlights</h3>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/null-61.png" width="188" height="128" alt="" title=""></p>
<p></p>
<p>Under <b>Teams</b> you will see the list of all your active teams.</p>
<p>Clicking on a team folder name will filter the <b>Highlights, </b>showing only those created and shared by that team.</p>
<h3>Deleting and Editing Highlights</h3>
<p>From the <b>Highlights </b>page, click on the menu located on the right hand side of a highlight. The options of “Delete” or “Edit” will be shown inside the menu.</p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/null-62.png" width="348" height="140" alt="" title=""></p>
<h4>Deleting a Highlight</h4>
<p>Click on “Delete.” A confirmation message will appear. Click “Ok.”</p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/null-63.png" width="612" height="186" alt="" title=""></p>
<p>The message will clear, and the highlight will be removed from your list.</p>
<h4>Editing a Highlight</h4>
<p>When you click on “Edit” in the menu, a modal will appear where you can change which folder the highlight is saved in. When you are satisfied with your change, click “Update Highlight.”</p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/null-64.png" width="624" height="221" alt="" title=""></p>
