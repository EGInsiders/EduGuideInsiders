<h1>1</h1>
<h2>Monitoring your Students’ Progress</h2>
<p></p>
<p>UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>EduGuide’s Metrics page is the best tool to turn to after your students have begun working on their path. From here, you can track students’ progress, review individual students’ comments, and respond directly to students.</p>
<p></p>
<p>In this video, I’ll review all of the features that you will want to know about as you stay on top of your students’ work on their paths.</p>
<p></p>
<p>---</p>

<p></p>
UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<h1>2</h1>
<h1>Navigating to your Team’s Metrics Page</h1>
<p></p>
<p>[Main Manu]()</p>
<p></p>
<h1></h1>
<h1>UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement</h1>
<p></p>
<p>To view the Metrics page, you will first want to click on the main menu control, then your team’s name, and then “Metrics.” You will be taken to a page which contains a list of the students who belong to the groups that you are currently following.</p>
<p></p>
<p>--</p>
<p>PAGE_BREAK: PageBreak</p>

UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<h1>3</h1>
<h1>Page Structure</h1>
<p></p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/null-6.png" width="408" height="69" alt="" title=""></p>
<p></p>
<h1></h1>
<h1>UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement</h1>
<p>The Metrics page is made of six main sections. Along the top of the page and on the left side, you will see your avatar and numbers that reflect the number of comments you have posted and the number of cheers you have sent to students. On the right-hand side, you will see a count of the number of activities that you have completed, and a button labeled “Continue” that will take you back into your path when you click it.</p>
<p></p>
<p>--</p>
UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<p>PAGE_BREAK: PageBreak</p>
<p></p>
UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<h1>4</h1>
<h2>Metric Views</h2>
<p></p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/null-16.png" width="408" height="341" alt="" title=""></p>
<p></p>
<h1></h1>
<h1>UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement</h1>
<p>The Metrics tool offers several different views that give you  different reports on how students or coaches are progressing in the path.</p>
<p></p>
<p>--</p>
<p>PAGE_BREAK: PageBreak</p>
UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<p></p>
UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<h1>5</h1>
<h2>Student Views</h2>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Activites</li>
<li>Coaching Received</li>
<li>Words Learned</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>By default, Metrics will show the Activities view. In this view, each student is listed with their path progress. Other views allow you to check on the number of comments students have received from coaches, and the words that students have defined via the literacy tools.</p>
<p></p>
<p>--</p>
UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<p>PAGE_BREAK: PageBreak</p>
<p></p>
UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<h2>6</h2>
<h2>Coach Views</h2>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Coaching Given</li>
<li>Time Invested</li>
<li>Setup</li>
<li>Coaches Joined</li>
<li>Coaches oriented</li>
<li>Coaches following Groups</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2></h2>
<h2>UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement</h2>
<p>You can also select views that present information on coaches’ progress. This includes the number of comments posted inside students’ paths, time invested, number of coaches who have joined a team, and the coaches’ progress in the PD Path.</p>
<p></p>
<p>--</p>
<p>PAGE_BREAK: PageBreak</p>
UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<h1>7</h1>
<p></p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/null-7.png" width="408" height="161" alt="" title=""></p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Along the bottom edge of the header you will see a number of filter and view controls. Starting at the right, you will see a control that allows you to change how the list of students is sorted. You can choose to sort the list by newest or oldest activity, most or least number of activities completed, or alphabetical order or reverse alphabetical order.</p>
<p></p>
<p>--</p>
UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<p>PAGE_BREAK: PageBreak</p>

<p></p>
<p>You can also adjust the view based on time frame. By default, the menu shows the choices of All Time, 28 days, and 7 days. You can also create a custom time range filter by entering in a start date and an end date. To do this, click on “Custom” . . .</p>
<p></p>

<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>. . . and then click on the starting and ending dates in your time range, then click “Update.”</p>
<p></p>
<p>--</p>
UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<p></p>

UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<h1>10</h1>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/null-10.png" width="408" height="201" alt="" title=""></p>
<p></p>
<h1></h1>
<h1>UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement</h1>
<p></p>
<p>By default, you will be shown only the students that are in the groups that you follow. However, you can make adjustments to your view of metrics by selecting a specific group. Click on “My Group Members” and then click on the name of the group you would like to track. The view will change so that it shows only the students in that group. If you need to manage the groups that you follow, click on “Manage Groups.”</p>
<p></p>
<p>You can also view students who have been archived by clicking on “Show Archived.”</p>
<p></p>
<p>--</p>
UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<p>PAGE_BREAK: PageBreak</p>
UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<h1>11</h1>
<p></p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/null-11.png" width="408" height="86" alt="" title=""></p>
<p></p>
<p>One last tool you can use is the search feature. You can find a student by entering in either their first or last name.</p>
<p></p>

UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<h1>13</h1>
<h2>## Managing Students</h2>
<p></p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/null-13.png" width="408" height="76" alt="" title=""></p>
<p></p>
<h2></h2>
<h2>UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement</h2>
<p>Students’ names and progress information are arranged as rows inside the main content area of the Metrics page. An option on the far right-hand side of each student’s progress bar allows you to take different actions.</p>
<p></p>
<p>--</p>
UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<h2>PAGE_BREAK: PageBreak</h2>
UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<h1>14</h1>
<h2>## Zooming in on Students</h2>
<p></p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/null-14.png" width="408" height="154" alt="" title=""></p>
<p></p>
<p>When you want to open up a detailed view of a student’s progress in the path, click on a student’s name. You will be taken to a page that shows the activities that the student has completed and their posted responses.</p>
<p></p>
<p>--</p>

UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement<h1>15</h1>
<p></p>
<p><img src="http://cyberland.gazerbeam.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/null-15.png" width="408" height="124" alt="" title=""></p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<h1></h1>
<h1>UNSUPPORTED: UnsupportedElement</h1>
<p></p>
<p>Click the plus icon to expand the list of steps underneath an activity. The plus icon will change to a minus sign when the list is expanded. Clicking on the minus sign will retract the list.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Within the list of steps, you can post a comment underneath the discussion steps. Click inside a blank comment field and then enter in your text.</p>
